Why TIG

The TIG Capital Difference

Bespoke Financing Structures

We do not offer off-the-shelf products. Every term sheet is crafted to the specific economics, risk profile, and timeline of the transaction. Our structures are built to close — not to conform to a template.

Relationship-Driven Lending

We build long-term partnerships with borrowers, advisors, and intermediaries. Our principals are directly involved in every transaction — from term sheet to funding. When you work with TIG, you work with decision-makers.

Speed and Certainty of Execution

Private capital should move at the speed of opportunity. We deliver term sheets in days, not weeks, and close with the certainty that comes from committed capital and a streamlined approval process.

Rigorous Due Diligence

Every transaction undergoes thorough independent analysis. We engage seasoned legal, accounting, and valuation professionals to ensure every credit decision is informed, defensible, and appropriately structured.

Discretion as Standard

We understand that borrowers seeking private capital often require confidentiality. Every inquiry and engagement is handled with institutional-grade discretion. Your business stays your business.
